Encoding: as in the verified artifact (`Workspace.Types.Equipartition`), the

artifact's exact-cardinality `IsEquipartitioned` (see bridge:equipartition).

def IsEquipartitionedGE (R : Finset ℕ) (m : ℕ) (Q : Finset ℕ) (T : ℕ) : Prop :=
  ∀ q ∈ Q, T ≤ (R.filter (fun i => m * q ≤ i ∧ i < m * (q + 1))).card
