descendant of the two-bit branch, has vector survivors exactly `B p`, retains a
`(1 - eta2)`-dense diagonal subset of the `alpha` column block (dominant/fuzzy
survival - exact block isolation is deliberately NOT asserted; the paper's
Stage-2 control only delivers the dense witness), leaves actual residual cost

expansion of the extracted local Stage-1 subgame on that dense diagonal set. -/
